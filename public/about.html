About Me

Hello there! I'm Oussama, a 22-year-old computer science enthusiast and passionate software developer currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in the vibrant city of Leicester, UK. My journey has been a global one, having been born in Tunisia, raised in the beautiful country of Italy, and obtaining my A-levels there. With the unwavering support of my parents, I made the significant decision to move to the UK for advanced education, driven by a passion for software development.

This multicultural exposure has not only broadened my perspectives but has also instilled in me a deep appreciation for diverse cultures and languages. I'm fluent in Arabic, Italian, and English, which allows me to seamlessly connect with people from different corners of the world. Beyond the realm of technology, I find joy in the simplicity of life's pleasures. I'm an avid football player, finding both challenge and camaraderie on the field. Cooking is another passion of mine, and I often find solace and creativity in the kitchen. When not immersed in code or sports, you'll likely find me absorbed in a good book, exploring the vast worlds that literature offers.

As a software developer, I am driven by a constant curiosity and a desire to create meaningful solutions. My decision to venture to the UK, leaving behind the familiar, was driven by a pursuit of better opportunities and personal growth. I aspire to leverage my educational background and experiences to establish a software company in the future, contributing to the ever-evolving tech landscape. This journey, although challenging, fuels my determination to make a meaningful impact and build a future where innovation and cultural exchange thrive.
