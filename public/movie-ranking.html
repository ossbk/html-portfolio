The Must-Watch Movies According to Oussama

My top 3 movies of all time

The Shawshank Redemption

An absolute classic! The storytelling and character development in this movie are unparalleled. A masterpiece by Frank Darabont.

Inception

Christopher Nolan's mind-bending masterpiece. The concept of dreams within dreams is brilliantly executed. A visually stunning and thought-provoking experience.

Pulp Fiction

Quentin Tarantino's non-linear storytelling at its finest. The unique narrative, memorable characters, and killer soundtrack make this film a cinematic gem.
